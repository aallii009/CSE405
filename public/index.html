Welcome

COVID-19 or the Coronavirus is virus that has many flu and pnemuina like symptoms, however, those symptoms are much stronger and are considered to be much more deadly than those of the flu. COVID-19 is especially harmful for at-risk indivduals such as the elderly and those with pre-existing medical conditions. In 2020, the World Health Organization declared the COVID-19 virus a global pandemic which has caused many governments around the world to issue the shutdown of schools and business as well as implementing quarantining for all people. New things about the virus are constantly being discovered by varius medical groups around the world who are looking for a vaccine to this disease.

Disclaimer: If you have any of the symptoms relating to COVID-19, please contact your doctor or any health care offical as to what to do next.
